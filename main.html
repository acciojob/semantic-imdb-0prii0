Chhichhore is a 2019 Hindi film that beautifully blends college nostalgia with an inspiring story about failure and friendship. The movie follows Anirudh (played by Sushant Singh Rajput), a middle-aged man whose son attempts suicide after failing an entrance exam. To help his son recover, Anirudh reunites with his college friends and shares stories of their time in college—how they were once labeled "losers" but learned to face challenges with courage and unity. The film delivers a strong message: failure is not the end, and what truly matters is how we deal with it. It mixes humor, emotions, and life lessons, making it a heartwarming and motivational watch.

Actors

Sushant Singh Rajput as Anirudh "Anni" Pathak – A caring father who recounts his college days to help his son understand the value of failure and friendship. Shraddha Kapoor as Maya Sharma Pathak – Anni’s college love and later his ex-wife; also part of the hostel gang. Varun Sharma as Gurmeet "Sexa" Singh Dhillon – The hilarious friend with a fixation on adult magazines; provides comic relief. Tahir Raj Bhasin as Derek D’Souza – The tough and rebellious senior who initially resents Anni but later becomes his close friend. Naveen Polishetty as Acid (Acidic) – A short-tempered, foul-mouthed guy who’s loyal to his friends. Tushar Pandey as Mummy (Sundar) – A shy, homesick boy who slowly grows in confidence with support from his friends. Saharsh Kumar Shukla as Bevda – The hostel drunkard who eventually finds purpose with the team. Prateik Babbar as Raggie – An arrogant athlete and the antagonist from the rival hostel. Mohammad Samad as Raghav Pathak – Anirudh and Maya’s teenage son who attempts suicide after failing an entrance exam.
